Loan Amortization Calculator
An Interactive Excel Financial Tool
By Triet Nguyen

Overview
This Excel-based loan amortization calculator allows users to input any loan amount, interest rate, and loan term to instantly generate a full monthly payment schedule. This was built to demonstrate practical Excel skills including financial formulas, structured data tables, and professional formatting.

Features

- Dynamic inputs, change loan amount, interest rate, or term and the entire schedule updates automatically
- Full 60-month payment schedule shows every monthly payment broken down by principal and interest
- Summary section displays total interest paid and total amount paid over the life of the loan
- Professional formatting, color-coded headers, alternating row colors, and currency formatting throughout


How to Use

1. Download the Loan_Amortization_Calculator.xlsx file
2. Open it in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets
3. Enter your values in the input section:
-Loan Amount = the total amount borrowed
-Annual Interest Rate = as a decimal (e.g. 0.05 for 5%)
-Loan Term (Years) = number of years to repay


The monthly payment and full amortization schedule will update automatically

Formulas Used
- PMT — calculates the fixed monthly payment
- SUM — calculates total interest and total amount paid
- Structured cell references with absolute referencing ($B$3) to ensure accuracy when copying formulas across 60 rows
